About Us

Gogte College of Commerce, established in 1954 in Belagavi, Karnataka, is one of the premier institutions in the field of commerce and management education. Managed by the Karnataka Law Society (KLS), the college has built a strong reputation for academic excellence, professional training, and holistic student development. Affiliated to Rani Channamma University, Belagavi, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs such as B.Com, BBA, MBA, and M.Com, along with other professional courses. It is well known for its disciplined environment, experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and active placement support, making it a preferred destination for aspiring students of commerce and management.

Vision

The vision of Gogte College of Commerce is to nurture and develop young minds into competent professionals and responsible citizens by providing quality education, value-based learning, and fostering innovation in commerce and management studies. The institution strives to create leaders who contribute positively to society and the global business environment.

Mission

The mission of Gogte College of Commerce is to impart excellent academic knowledge in commerce and management while instilling values of discipline, leadership, and social responsibility. The college aims to provide opportunities for skill enhancement, research, and entrepreneurship so that students are empowered to meet global challenges with confidence and competence.
